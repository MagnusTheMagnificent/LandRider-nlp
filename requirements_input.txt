The Land Raider shall use the ultrasonic sensor to detect obstacles within a distance of 10 cm
The Land Raider shall stop the drive motors within the avoidance threshold of 0.2 seconds after obstacle detection
The Land Raider shall command the drive motors to turn the Land Raider at least 30 degrees away from the detected obstacle
The Land Raider shall resume forward motion after obstacle clearance is confirmed by the ultrasonic sensor
The Land Raider shall ensure the microcontroller executes the obstacle avoidance algorithm every 0.5s
The Land Raider shall monitor battery state and prevent further movement if battery voltage is below 6.0V
The Robot shall use onboard LEDs to indicate when an obstacle has triggered an avoidance maneuver
The Robot shall allow users to upload updated obstacle avoidance scripts via USB or Bluetooth
The Robot shall stop movement if any component critical to obstacle avoidance is malfunctioning
